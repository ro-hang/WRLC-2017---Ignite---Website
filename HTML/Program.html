Workshops

Workshops are an essential part of any conference, and WRLC will be no exception! The categories for the workshops this year will be:

Business and Industry

Gain insight from some of the top business leaders in their industries and how to make it to the top!

Leadership Development

Want to become the best leader possible? There will be a variety to leadership workshops you can use to help become the best version of yourself!

Competition Training

Do you have what it takes to make it to ICDC in Atlanta, Georgia? We will have competition workshops so that you will be able to!

Networking              

It's not always about what you know, but who you know! Learn some of the best networking tactics that exist in the professional world!
